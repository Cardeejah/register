Our Services Just For You

Ensuring security, optimum care and healthy childhood memories

Baby Care

Baby care is our priority. Under this category, we provide you with a nanny whose main aim is to watch over and and take care of your child in your absence. These nannies have been evaluated mentally, physically and psychologically. We guarantee the security and safety of your child(ren) with them. They come with years of experience and have been trained in child psychology and behaviour. This makes them the best fit for you if all you need is optimum care for your wards.

Home services

As a mother or guardian, we understand that you may need extra services from your Nanny. This is why we have the home services category. These categories of Nanny will help you with manual chores at home, like cleaning, cooking or helping with your pile of laundry. They come in handy when you don't want to have a separate maid for cleaning and baby care. They also take care of your babies, professionally and they are excellent home makers.

Creche Nanny

As a school owner, it may be difficult to get a smart, learned and dedicated nanny to look over your creche babies. We understand this, that is why we have Nannies specifically for schools. These nannies are well trained for school activities and the best part is, you get to work with them on a contract basis. This eradicates the need to hire a permanent staff which could be costly. Our creche nannies are knowledgeable and equipped with the latest technological advancements, which makes it easier to play with children while utilizing the gadgets that you may provide. They will help look after your creche kids in the most healthy, educative and fun way possible!.
